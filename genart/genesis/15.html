Day 15, "Growth". I had this theme seeded before I knew how I wanted to express it and then I stumbled on the phrase "differential line growth" - seemed relevant. As I explored the algorithm I detected a lot of overlap with the force directed graph layout I played with in graph and the flocking beaviour in emerge.

Typical implementations initiialize with a simple line or circle but I used the polyline from shape. I also reused the fill logic - when you are satisfied with the shape of the curve you can pause the growth and have it paint the interior.
